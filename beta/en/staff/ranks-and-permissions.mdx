This section defines the **official hierarchical structure** of the CosmixMC Network Staff, as well as the **responsibilities, permissions, and operational limits** associated with each rank.

Ranks exist to **organize work, define responsibilities, and prevent abuse**, not to grant personal privileges.

Every Staff member must act **strictly within the scope of their rank**.

---

## Administrative Team

**Role:** Overall server management.

**Responsibilities:**

- Final and partial decision-making.
- Global management of Staff and server.
- Approval of structural changes, regulations, and severe sanctions.
- Resolution of high-level internal conflicts.

---

## Manager

**Role:** Operational Staff management.

**Responsibilities:**

- Direct management of the Staff Team.
- Performance evaluation.
- Proposal of promotions, internal sanctions, and reorganizations.
- Coordination with the Administrative Team.

**Limits:**

- Cannot override decisions made by the Administrative Team.

---

## Community Manager

**Role:** Community and communication management.

**Responsibilities:**

- Management of Discord and internal platforms.
- Official communication with the community.
- Supervision of the overall environment.
- Coordination with Moderation and Managers.

**Limits:**

- Cannot issue sanctions outside what is established by Moderation/Admins.

---

## Operator

**Role:** Authorized technical and operational management.

**Responsibilities:**

- Technical support for infrastructure.
- Management of technical tools.
- Assistance with panel or server incidents.
- Installation of key systems.

**Limits:**

- Cannot make disciplinary decisions without authorization.

---

## Development Team

**Role:** Development and maintenance of game modes.

**Responsibilities:**

- Development of systems, plugins, and tools.
- Technical maintenance of the server.
- Internal technical support for Staff.

**Limits:**

- Does not intervene in moderation or sanctions unless explicitly authorized.

---

## Sr. Admin / Admin / Jr. Admin

**Role:** Server administration.

**Responsibilities:**

- Advanced server management.
- Handling appeals.
- Supervision of Moderation.
- Enforcement of severe sanctions according to regulations.

**Limits:**

- Operate under coordination of higher ranks.

---

## Sr. Mod / Mod / Jr. Mod

**Role:** Active moderation.

**Responsibilities:**

- Enforcement of sanctions according to the guide.
- Management of moderation tickets.
- Resolution of user conflicts.
- Supervision of Helpers.

**Limits:**

- Cannot make administrative decisions.
- Severe cases must be escalated.

---

## DcMod

**Role:** Discord-specific moderation.

**Responsibilities:**

- Moderation of Discord channels.
- Enforcement of sanctions on Discord.
- Ensuring compliance with rules.

**Limits:**

- Does not intervene in Minecraft-related sanctions.

---

## Helper

**Role:** Basic support and assistance.

**Responsibilities:**

- Initial user support.
- Answering questions.
- Escalating cases to Moderation.

**Limits:**

- Cannot issue permanent sanctions.
- Does not make final decisions.

---

## Support

**Role:** User assistance and support.

**Responsibilities:**

- Handling support tickets.
- Player guidance.
- Clear and professional communication.

**Limits:**

- Does not issue sanctions or intervene in moderation.

---

## Special Ranks

**Staff of the Month / Staff of the Week**

- Internal recognition.
- **Do not grant additional permissions**.
- Do not imply authority over other members.

---

## Misuse of Permissions

The following are considered misuse, among others:

- Acting outside of one's rank.
- Abusing permissions.
- Using the role for personal benefit.
- Making decisions without proper authority.

Such behavior may result in **internal sanctions**, including immediate removal of the rank.
