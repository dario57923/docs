This section outlines the **internal tools used by CosmixMC Network Staff**, along with their basic usage and best practices.

Proper use of these tools is **essential** to ensure efficient, organized, and secure server management.

---

## General Principles

- Staff tools must be used **exclusively for role-related purposes**.
- Misuse, abuse, or improper use may result in sanctions.
- In case of doubt about a tool, it must be clarified before use.
- Not all tools are available to all ranks.

---

## Administration Panel

**Purpose:** Technical server management.

**Main functions:**

- Server restarts.
- File management.
- Resource monitoring.
- Instance control.

**Best practices:**

- Do not restart servers without a justified reason.
- Avoid making changes without proper knowledge.
- Report important incidents.

---

## Ticket System

**Purpose:** User support and assistance.

**Main functions:**

- Handling user requests.
- Resolving questions and issues.
- Escalating cases.

**Best practices:**

- Respond clearly, formally, and professionally.
- Do not close tickets without resolution or confirmation.
- Avoid ambiguous or incomplete responses.

---

## Moderation Bots

**Purpose:** Enforcement of sanctions and server control.

**Main functions:**

- Muting, kicking, or banning users.
- Logging sanctions.
- Moderation automation.

**Best practices:**

- Always apply sanctions according to the regulations.
- Review actions before executing them.
- Avoid impulsive sanctions.

---

## Internal Staff Channels

**Purpose:** Internal team communication.

**Main functions:**

- Asking questions.
- Staff coordination.
- Incident reporting.

**Best practices:**

- Keep conversations relevant and professional.
- Avoid unnecessary channel clutter.
- Use the appropriate channel for each situation.

---

## Internal Documentation

**Purpose:** Primary reference for Staff actions.

**Main functions:**

- Consulting regulations.
- Procedures and guides.
- Training resources.

**Best practices:**

- Consult before acting when in doubt.
- Stay up to date.
- Do not share outside the Staff.

---

## Misuse of Tools

The following are considered misuse, among others:

- Using tools without justified reason.
- Executing actions without proper knowledge.
- Abusing permissions.
- Using tools for personal or third-party benefit.

Such behavior may result in **internal sanctions**, including removal of the rank.

---

## Staff Responsibility

Every Staff member is responsible for:

- Knowing the tools they have access to.
- Using them correctly.
- Acting with judgment and responsibility.
- Requesting help when in doubt.
