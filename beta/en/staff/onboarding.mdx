This section is intended for **any member joining the CosmixMC Network Staff**, regardless of their rank.\
Reading and complying with it is **mandatory** before starting any duties.

---

## Onboarding Objective

The onboarding process aims to:

- Ensure all Staff members understand the internal regulations.
- Prevent mistakes caused by lack of knowledge.
- Standardize operational criteria from day one.
- Ensure proper integration into the Team.

## During Your First Days

During your initial period as Staff:

- Observe how more experienced Staff members handle situations.
- Ask any questions before acting — it is better to ask than to make mistakes.
- Avoid intervening in complex cases without authorization if you lack the necessary knowledge.
- Always prioritize proper treatment of users.

> **Note:** Making mistakes due to rushing is sanctionable. Asking before acting never is.

---

## Communication and Coordination

- Use the appropriate internal channels for questions or doubts.
- Do not question decisions in front of users.
- Maintain clear, formal, and respectful language.
- Avoid public discussions or arguments in tickets.

---

## Commitment and Responsibility

Being part of the Staff implies:

- Commitment to the project.
- Respect for the established hierarchy.
- Absolute confidentiality.
- Minimum availability and proper justification of absences.
