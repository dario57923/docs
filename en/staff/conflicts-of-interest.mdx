This section regulates the **prevention, identification, and management of conflicts of interest** within the MotionMC Network Staff, with the objective of ensuring **impartiality, transparency, and professionalism** in all actions.

Every Staff member must always prioritize the interests of the server and the community over any personal interests.

---

## What is a Conflict of Interest?

A conflict of interest is any situation in which a Staff member:

- Obtains or may obtain a personal benefit.
- Acts in a partial or biased manner.
- Has a relationship that may influence their judgment.
- Compromises the objectivity of a decision.

The existence of a conflict **does not automatically imply an infraction**, but **failing to manage it properly does**.

---

## General Principles

- Staff must always act impartially, regardless of friendships or personal relationships with users or other Staff members.
- Transparency is mandatory.
- In case of doubt, the situation must be reported and escalated to the Head of Staff or Administration.
- Concealing a conflict is considered a serious infraction.

---

## Situations Considered as Conflicts of Interest

The following are considered conflicts of interest, among others:

- Moderating, sanctioning, or handling cases involving:
  - Close friends.
  - Partners.
  - Family members.
  - Individuals with whom there is a direct personal relationship.
- Directly or indirectly benefiting known users.
- Making decisions that may favor one’s own position, rank, or image.
- Participating in evaluations, sanctions, or promotions where a personal relationship exists.
- Using internal information for personal or third-party benefit.

## Procedure in Case of a Conflict of Interest

When a Staff member detects or believes that a conflict of interest may exist, they must:

1. **Refrain from intervening** in the case.
2. Report the situation to a higher rank.
3. Provide the necessary information privately.
4. Follow the instructions received.

> Acting without reporting a conflict will be considered a serious infraction and you will be considered complicit.

---

## Conflicts Between Staff Members

- Personal conflicts between Staff members must not affect server management.
- Any disagreement must be handled privately and with a superior.
- Using one’s rank to harm another Staff member is strictly prohibited.

---

## Sanctions for Conflicts of Interest

Failure to comply with this policy may result in:

- One or more Strikes.
- Temporary removal of the rank.
- Permanent removal from the Staff in serious or repeated cases.

Severity will be assessed based on impact and intent.

---

## Individual Responsibility

Every Staff member is responsible for:

- Identifying potential conflicts.
- Acting with honesty and transparency.
- Always prioritizing the interests of the server.
- Consulting before acting when in doubt.
